About

tell-me-everything is a very simple flask app for anonymous users to post their thoughts on "The Wall"!
It uses flask as the backend framework and sqlite for database. It is currently deployed on heroku obviously!
It might be sometimes that your post is not sent, so resend if it does not show up on the wall!

Type in an anonymous username and speak your mind! Please don't spam, as I have not yet implemented the spam filter. Lol.
